UX Design a Study

We then presented to our class our findings. This was a valuable experience because we were able to learn how to run data analytics in Microsoft Excel, and how to create a study from scratch.

This kind of testing will be beneficial to me as a marketer because it gives insight on consumer behaviors, and helps refine my A/B testing skills and perfect my research abilities.
